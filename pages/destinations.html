Maasai Mara National Reserve

Home to the legendary Great Migration, the Maasai Mara is Kenya's crown jewel. Witness over 2 million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles cross the treacherous Mara River in one of nature's most spectacular events.

Trip Highlights:

  • ✓Great Migration (July-October)
  • ✓Big Five game viewing
  • ✓Maasai cultural experiences
  • ✓Hot air balloon safaris

Best Visiting Season

July to October for the Great Migration, December to March for excellent weather and game viewing.

Diani Beach

Consistently rated among Africa's best beaches, Diani offers 25 kilometers of pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs perfect for snorkeling and diving.

Trip Highlights:

  • ✓World-class snorkeling & diving
  • ✓Dhow sunset cruises
  • ✓Swahili cultural experiences
  • ✓Colobus monkey sanctuary

Best Visiting Season

October to March for dry weather, June to September for cooler temperatures and excellent diving conditions.

Mount Kenya

Africa's second-highest mountain offers diverse ecosystems from tropical rainforest to alpine desert. The journey to Point Lenana (4,985m) passes through breathtaking landscapes and unique wildlife.

Trip Highlights:

  • ✓Point Lenana summit (4,985m)
  • ✓Diverse ecosystems
  • ✓Unique alpine wildlife
  • ✓Spectacular glacial valleys

Best Visiting Season

January-March and July-October for clear weather and best summit conditions.

Amboseli National Park

Famous for its large elephant herds and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro, Amboseli offers some of Africa's most iconic safari photography opportunities against the backdrop of Africa's highest peak.

Trip Highlights:

  • ✓Large elephant herds
  • ✓Mount Kilimanjaro views
  • ✓Maasai cultural visits
  • ✓Diverse birdlife (400+ species)

Best Visiting Season

June to October and January to March for clear Kilimanjaro views and excellent game viewing.

Lake Nakuru National Park

A birdwatcher's paradise famous for its massive flocks of flamingos that turn the lake pink. This compact park is also a rhino sanctuary and offers excellent wildlife viewing in a stunning setting.

Trip Highlights:

  • ✓Flamingo spectacle
  • ✓Rhino sanctuary
  • ✓Over 450 bird species
  • ✓Spectacular viewpoints

Best Visiting Season

Year-round destination, but July to January offers the best flamingo viewing and weather conditions.
